About Us

The Asantehene Lands Secretariat is a public office in Kumasi, Ghana, responsible for managing and administering stool lands under the Asantehene, Otumfuo Osei Tutu II. We handle land-related matters for the Asante traditional area, including land acquisition, documentation, and dispute resolution

Our Services

  • Land Acquisition and Documentation

    The secretariat handles processes related to acquiring land form the Asantehene, including documentation, registration and ensuring proper land transactions

  • Dispute Resolution and Arbitration

    We play a role in resolving land disputes within the Asante traditional area.

  • Information and Consultation

    The Secretariat provides information and consultations to the public on land-related matters, and these services are often free.

  • Land Use Planning

    We contribute to the sustainable and judicious use of land within the Asante traditional area, ensuring development aligns with national goals.

  • Engaging Stakeholders

    The Secretariat collabrates with traditional authorities, landowners, and government agencies to manage land effectively.

  • Monitoring and Reporting

    We prepare reports on operations and activities, providing information to the Asantehene, land-owning groups, and other stakeholders
